TRAVELING GUIDE

in 2018, the Would Be Wanderer Foundation (fka Never Not Doing Things) was officially recognized as a 501c3 Charitable Non-Profit Organization. Since its inception, Would Be Wanderer Foundation has connected thousands of people over a shared appreciation of adventure, travel and culture – offering education into and opportunities inspired by the World around us, specifically for those who may otherwise go without. Through volunteer participation and donations, the team has been able to offer hundreds of hosted/guided events, from multi-day backpacking seminars to camping and hiking trips, to local tours and education in the US and abroad.In addition, Would Be Wanderer Foundation’s park and trail cleanup efforts have, thus far, accounted for over 1500 gallons of trash removed from local parks across the US.
